The Concept

From End of the Line to Top of Its Game

We partner with rail operators worldwide to rescue decommissioned train cabins, then refurbish them into beautifully designed micro-workspaces for 4–10 people — stationed in parks, plazas, waterfronts, and neighbourhoods across the city.

Decommissioned & Refurbished

Retired rail cabins get a second life. We strip them back, reinforce the shell, and rebuild the interior to premium workspace standards — sustainable, unique, and built to last.

4–10 Person Workspaces

Each cabin is an intimate, self-contained workspace — perfect for small teams, freelancers, or focused solo work. Stationed right where you need them, across the city.

Global Rail Partnerships

We work with rail operators on every continent to source retired stock — giving new purpose to old carriages and generating revenue for the rail network.
